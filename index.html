How to export?

Steps on How to Export Butterflies

Step 1

Account Registration

Sign Up if you are a new user for you to have an account, but if you already have just sign in your account.

Step 2

Apply for New Permit

Create and fill up the needed information in the application form, then after that submit it.

Step 3

Permit Status Monitoring

Wait for the status of your appllication, if the your application was returned edit it based on the remarks given by the admin, but if your application was accepted go to the office and present the butterfly to be transported.

Step 4

Export the Butterfly

If the butterfly are all goods, you can now pay for the transport fee. After that you can now get your Local Transport Permit and use it to transport the butterfly
